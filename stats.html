Cost analysis

We would want to make some assumptions.

  • First, when we say a normal bungalow, we mean a three bed room bungalow covering about 50 metres by 50 metres.
  • Then, a storey building would normally be a standard duplex covering about 80metres by 80metres

Lintel This is a step that takes the least amount of money. The materials involved include garvel, sharp sand and iron rods. Lets say maybe 70k for bungalow and above 200k for storey building
Deck The deck is always expensive if it isa concrete deck. A wooden deck won't cost more than 100k but a concrete deck absorbs at least 500k, naturally a concrete deck is 6 inches thick. We may say that a bag of cement can get a coverage of about 5ft by 5ft or 1.5m x1.5m.
Roofing The rafter skeleton alone takes up almost half of the money. The materials and workmanship cost about 150k for a bungalow and 600k for a duplex.
Plastering The number of bags of cement is always very high and the cement sand would also be many trips. The price of sand is varied widely from location to location in Nigeria. So I would advise that you use the calculator to get how much.
Plumbing This Phase will surely take up at least 500k if you need a sumo, a couple tanks or reservours and every other pipelines. But if it is only drainage, it could be accomplished with some 100k.
Electrical Fittings Wiring house is normally a contract of 40k per flat in Aba. But it may vary widely over other Geographical settings. The cost of the materials for the electrical is roughly 50k for bungalow and 200k for storey buildings.
Painting Painting a room should cost about 2k and the cost of painting the external walls would equal the cost of painting the internals divided by three. I advise that you use the calculator provided to ascertain how much you will spend on them.
Decoration The interior decoration is the brain child of the interior decorator and the amount to be spent will depend largely on the taste of the owner. Bear in mind that this is a part where most people like to skip steps for the sake of saving money. My advice is that you save 500k if you have a bungalow and a high taste and 2million if you are trying to decorate a duplex to sparkling standards.
Finishing Every other miscellaneous expenditure goes here. These would include swimming pool, house lawns, flower planting, and other stuff to enhance luxury. This step is oftten always skipped.(smiles...).
